## Background

Elizabeth is the wife of [[NPCs/Fidelity Investments/Edward Johnson III\|Edward Johnson III]], chairman of Fidelity Investments. She holds trusteeships with both the **Museum of Fine Arts, Boston** and the **Winterthur Museum** (Delaware) — which means she carries a personal **security keycard for the MFA** as part of her duties.

In late October 1986, Elizabeth and her husband were guests at the **Park Plaza Hotel**.

---

## Session 02 — Lee's First Mark

[[Player Characters/Lee Smith\|Lee]] entered the Johnsons' suite during the day, wearing a stolen housekeeping uniform. He inspected Elizabeth's handbag and found two items of interest:

- A **pearl necklace** valued at approximately $150, with her initials engraved on the clasp. Lee passed — the initials would make fencing it traceable.
- A **security keycard for the Museum of Fine Arts, Boston.** Lee took this.

Elizabeth has not yet noticed the keycard is missing. No alarm has been raised.

## Real-World Note

Elizabeth "Lilly" Johnson is a real public figure — wife of Ned Johnson III, philanthropist, and trustee of multiple cultural institutions including the MFA. This campaign uses her as an NPC in the 1986 Boston setting.
