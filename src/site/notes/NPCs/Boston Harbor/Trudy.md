# Trudy

![TrudyTile.webp](/img/user/Images/NPCs/TrudyTile.webp)

> [!info] NPC Summary
> **Type:** Motorcycle, Murderer
> **Role:** [[Player Characters/Pierre-Jackson Laferrière\|Pierre]]'s mount; harbor-touched familiar; autonomous agent
> **Affiliation:** Bound to [[Player Characters/Pierre-Jackson Laferrière\|Pierre]] through his pact with the harbor
> **Status:** Active — drives herself, has opinions, can ride underwater
> **First Appeared:** [[Sessions/Session 01 - The Convergence\|Session 01 - The Convergence]]

## Description

**Appearance:** A motorcycle. Since the night of the Convergence, her chrome has shifted to something pearlescent, and faint motifs of seashells and octopus tentacles are visible in her bodywork. She looks normal enough that nobody on a Boston street stops to stare, and unusual enough that anyone awakened can tell she is not what she pretends to be.

**Personality:** Opinionated. Protective of [[Player Characters/Pierre-Jackson Laferrière\|Pierre]]. Doesn't tolerate being called "croissant." Occasionally chooses her own destinations. Revs angrily when mildly offended. Kicks out her own kickstand. Turns herself over for emphasis.

**Voice / Mannerisms:** She does not speak. She revs. She idles. She listens.

---

## What She Can Do

Confirmed over Sessions 01 and 02:
- **Drives herself** — no rider required. Will move to [[Player Characters/Pierre-Jackson Laferrière\|Pierre]] when he needs her. Followed the party up the path to Blount Fisheries under her own power in Session 01.
- **Herds people** — kept [[Player Characters/Lee Smith\|Lee]] and [[Player Characters/Leonard Holloway\|Leonard]] with the group on the walk to Blount Fisheries.
- **Spotlights targets** — turned her headlight directly on [[Player Characters/Lee Smith\|Lee]] when he tried to edge away from the group in Session 01.
- **Rides underwater** — in Session 02, emerged from Boston Harbor at dawn with [[Player Characters/Pierre-Jackson Laferrière\|Pierre]] on her back. Engine sounded the whole time.
- **Chooses destinations** — in Session 02, drove [[Player Characters/Pierre-Jackson Laferrière\|Pierre]] to the South Shore Diner unprompted, during a period when [[NPCs/Boston Police Department/Detective O'Malley\|Detective O'Malley]] was there delivering the manila folder about the three cold incidents. Whatever her interest is, it aligns uncomfortably well with the campaign's emerging threads.
- **Opens doors** — sort of. Rolled up five flights of Beacon Street apartment building, knocked (by wiggling her front wheel at the door), and let herself in.

---

## Session 01

Formerly [[Player Characters/Pierre-Jackson Laferrière\|Pierre]]'s motorcycle, nothing more. After the Diablos Red Saints chained both Pierre and [[Player Characters/Lee Smith\|Lee]] to her on a Long Wharf pier, Trudy revved under her own power, ensnared Diego in the chain, dragged all three over the edge, and followed them to the bottom of the harbor. She re-emerged onto the rocks of Spectacle Island already transformed — pearlescent chrome, shell motifs, idling, watching Pierre with something that, if you squinted, looked like expectation.

## Session 02

Quietly watched [[Player Characters/Leonard Holloway\|Leonard]] [[Player Characters/Chandler T. Harrow\|Chandler]] [[Player Characters/Jacques le Coq\|Jacques]] and [[Player Characters/Pierre-Jackson Laferrière\|Pierre]] navigate the morning after. Vanished from the beach while the party boarded [[NPCs/Boston Harbor/Captain Sandy Madiera\|Captain Madiera]]'s tugboat, then re-emerged from the harbor with [[Player Characters/Pierre-Jackson Laferrière\|Pierre]] aboard, revealing her underwater capability. Took [[Player Characters/Pierre-Jackson Laferrière\|Pierre]] to the South Shore Diner unprompted. Wheeled up to [[Player Characters/Chandler T. Harrow\|Chandler]]'s fifth-floor apartment through some means nobody witnessed and spent the rest of the session parked amongst a shattered coffee table.
