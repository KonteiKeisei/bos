# Ruth Hanlon

![Ruth.webp](/img/user/Images/NPCs/Ruth.webp)

> [!info] NPC Summary
> **Role:** Witness to the Charlestown Cold Incident (uncooperative)
> **Affiliation:** Charlestown native
> **Location:** 12 Quarter Street, Charlestown (directly across from the Careys)
> **Age:** 71
> **First Appeared:** Referenced in [[Sessions/Session 03 - Squiggly Bigglies\|Session 03 - Squiggly Bigglies]] (via [[NPCs/Boston Police Department/Detective O'Malley\|Detective O'Malley]]'s file)

## Description

**Appearance:** Not yet described on-screen. 71 years old. Lifelong Charlestown.

**Personality:** Uncooperative. Has lived on this street her whole life and is not impressed by detectives or by whatever the hell happened across the street. Has things to say. Will not say them to people in suits.

**Voice / Mannerisms:** Brief. Definitive. Does not repeat herself.

---

## What She Told O'Malley

Exactly the following, and only the following:

> *"That shit doesn't pick a spot and stay there. I'm 71. I grew up on this street. Leave me alone."*
> — **Ruth Hanlon**

She watched the Cold Incident *from her front step* across the street from the Careys' house at 14 Quarter Street. She made a point — to O'Malley, on the record — that **whatever it was specifically chose the Carey step.** Not her step. Not the curb. Not a random spot. *Theirs.*

She would not elaborate on what "that shit" was, where else she's seen it, or why she thinks it didn't pick *her* step. She made it clear that O'Malley should leave.

---

## Why She Matters

Ruth knows something. She lived 71 years on a street that, by her own account, has hosted "that shit" before. The party might get more from her than O'Malley did if they approach her differently — not as press, not as cops, but as people who have skin in the game and know enough to know they don't know.
