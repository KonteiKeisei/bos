**Appearance:** Not yet described on-screen. Wife of [[NPCs/Charlestown/Robert Carey\|Robert Carey]], mother of [[NPCs/Charlestown/Kelsey Carey\|Kelsey Carey]].

**Personality:** Not yet established. Was present in the living room during the Cold Incident on October 31. Has not given an independent statement to [[NPCs/Boston Police Department/Detective O'Malley\|Detective O'Malley]] — only her husband and daughter were quoted.

---

## Background

Sandra is part of the Carey family household at 14 Quarter Street in Charlestown. She watched Game 7 with her husband and daughter on October 27 — the same game-watching pattern that lines up with every other Cold Incident victim. On **October 31, 1986**, she went under during the incident along with her husband and daughter.

She is not on the record yet. The party has not interviewed her.
