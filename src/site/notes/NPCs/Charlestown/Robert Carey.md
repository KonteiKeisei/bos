# Robert Carey

![RobertCarey.webp](/img/user/Images/NPCs/RobertCarey.webp)

> [!info] NPC Summary
> **Role:** Longshoreman; survivor of the Charlestown Cold Incident
> **Affiliation:** Boston waterfront labor
> **Location:** 14 Quarter Street, Charlestown
> **Age:** 44
> **First Appeared:** Referenced in [[Sessions/Session 03 - Squiggly Bigglies\|Session 03 - Squiggly Bigglies]] (via [[NPCs/Boston Police Department/Detective O'Malley\|Detective O'Malley]]'s file)

## Description

**Appearance:** Big guy, quiet, that kind of man. O'Malley's three-word characterization.

**Personality:** A working man who tells it straight. Watches games with his family. Did not see what came into the room from the corner — but felt it, and *knew* it knew where it was going.

**Voice / Mannerisms:** Plainspoken. Tries to give an honest description of something he doesn't have the vocabulary for.

---

## Background

Robert is a Boston harbor longshoreman, married, household of three at 14 Quarter Street in Charlestown. On the night of **October 31, 1986**, he was watching a Channel 4 Game 7 recap on television with his wife [[NPCs/Charlestown/Sandra Carey\|Sandra Carey]] and his daughter [[NPCs/Charlestown/Kelsey Carey\|Kelsey Carey]] when the temperature in their living room dropped *not from a draft, but from a corner.*

---

## What He Told O'Malley

> *"The cold came from the corner of where the TV was. Not a draft. From the corner. It felt like something came in that doesn't need a window. The cold had a shape. Couldn't see it clear, but the way it moved, it wasn't like air. It knew where it was going."*
> — **Robert Carey**

He didn't see a shape. He felt one. The family went under — *sluggish, foggy, distant* — for some indeterminate stretch. When they came back, the cold was gone. They were physically unharmed.

He doesn't know what attacked his family. He knows *something* did. He called the police the next morning.

> [!note] What the party hasn't asked him yet
> Robert has not been interviewed by the party in person. They have only read his statement in [[NPCs/Boston Police Department/Detective O'Malley\|Detective O'Malley]]'s manila folder. If they go to 14 Quarter Street, he's there.
