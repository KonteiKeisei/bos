# The Grief-Eater

![Grief Eater.webp](/img/user/Images/NPCs/Grief%20Eater.webp)

> [!info] NPC Summary
> **Type:** Minor Fey Entity
> **Role:** Session 01 Antagonist
> **Status:** Destroyed — [[Sessions/Session 01 - The Convergence\|Session 01 - The Convergence]]
> **First Appeared:** [[Sessions/Session 01 - The Convergence\|Session 01 - The Convergence]]

## Description

**Appearance:** A too-long neck bending at angles necks don't bend. A face that had once been human and had decided to stop. Tattered fabric that seems made of condensed shadow. Frost and flower petals drifting constantly around its form — a sign of both Courts' influence.

**Voice:** Like someone trying to speak while sobbing — broken, wet, gasping.

**First impression from the party:**
> *"It looks like the end of a finger with eyes on it, man."*
> — [[Player Characters/Bohdan Thorruk\|Bo]]

---

## Nature

The Grief-Eater is not malevolent by nature — it is simply **hungry**, and Boston in the wake of the World Series loss is a feast. It slipped through a crack in the veil opened by the city's collective despair.

**Behavior:**
- Feeds on grief, capturing living sources of emotional energy
- [[Player Characters/Bohdan Thorruk\|Bo]]'s nature check (19, advantage on fungi) revealed: it generates grief rather than absorbing it — antithetical to the Luminous Hollow organisms
- Pinned three Red Sox fans to the wall of Blount Fisheries with dark fungal growth, feeding on them
- Used **Wail of the Bereaved** as its signature attack — an area effect that caused Frightened condition and dealt lingering injuries
- Struck with **Sorrow Lash**, a melee attack that shredded clothing and pads but repeatedly missed [[Player Characters/Jacques le Coq\|Jacques]]
- When cornered, leapt from 15 feet of machinery — failed its landing, went prone

---

## Combat — Session 01

The Grief-Eater was fought by the full party inside Blount Fisheries.

**Notable moments:**
- Its Wail of the Bereaved (Round 1) dealt permanent or extended lingering injuries to four party members: [[Player Characters/Pierre-Jackson Laferrière\|Pierre]] (migraine), [[Player Characters/Bohdan Thorruk\|Bo]] (nosebleeds), [[Player Characters/Leonard Holloway\|Leonard]] (permanent minor illusion on right ring finger), and [[Player Characters/Lee Smith\|Lee]] (lost the ability to speak entirely)
- Healed [[Player Characters/Chandler T. Harrow\|Chandler]]'s 1 damage back as a reaction by feasting on his grief
- Fell prone after failing an acrobatics check jumping from the machinery
- Was destroyed by [[Player Characters/Jacques le Coq\|Jacques]] with a Divine Smite slap shot to the jaw

**The killing blow:**
> *"I see it start to open its mouth. And I'm like, no, no, no, no, no. And I slap shot it right across the jaw. Whack! Dislocate its mouth."*
> — [[Player Characters/Jacques le Coq\|Jacques]]

The bottom half of its face sailed across the facility and landed in a vat of rotted fish bones.

---

## Last Words

As it came apart — threads of shadow, frost crystallizing and shattering, flower petals blooming and dying — it spoke in a language only [[Player Characters/Bohdan Thorruk\|Bo]] understood. His translation:

> *"The harvest was denied. The courts will remember. Winter judges. Summer rages. The Green One dreams of walking…"*
