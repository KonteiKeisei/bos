# Diane Okoye

![DianeOkoye.webp](/img/user/Images/NPCs/DianeOkoye.webp)

> [!info] NPC Summary
> **Role:** Paranormal Investigator, Boston Paranormal Society
> **Affiliation:** Boston Paranormal Society
> **Location:** Boston — currently hospitalised
> **First Appeared:** [[Sessions/Session 01 - The Convergence\|Session 01 - The Convergence]]

## Description

**Appearance:** Late twenties, sharp-eyed and quick-handed. Always carrying something she built herself — a sensor, a recorder, a modification of a modification. Looks more comfortable with a soldering iron than a conversation.

**Personality:** Scientific rigour first, wonder second — though the wonder is clearly there underneath if you look. Takes her equipment seriously. Takes the work seriously. Genuinely believes in what the Society is trying to do.

**Voice / Mannerisms:** Precise and technical. Measures things. Always measuring things.

---

## Background

Diane has been [[Player Characters/Leonard Holloway\|Leonard]]'s closest ally in the Boston Paranormal Society — the one who actually shows up to investigate rather than just discuss. On the night of October 10th, she was with Leonard at Long Wharf, monitoring anomalous electromagnetic readings that had been off the charts for three days straight.

[[NPCs/Long Wharf/The Ghost Captain\|A ghost]] walked through her body. The frost it left behind was distributed in fractal patterns across her skin.

She is currently hospitalised. Her condition is stable, but she has not regained full responsiveness.

---

## Session 01

Diane was recording EM data at Long Wharf when [[NPCs/Long Wharf/The Ghost Captain\|The Ghost Captain]] manifested — a soldier in period uniform, a smear of distortion with eyes like holes punched through the air. It passed directly through her body, leaving her covered in frost and immediately unconscious. Her last words before going down:

> *"Leonard, are you getting the—"*

[[Player Characters/Leonard Holloway\|Leonard]] caught her before she fell into the water. Emergency services were called by a dock worker who witnessed the scene. Diane was loaded into an ambulance.

Her recording equipment captured the EM spike immediately before the manifestation — the data survived even though the device cracked. [[NPCs/Boston Police Department/Detective O'Malley\|Detective O'Malley]] has the device.

## Session 02 — Ten Days Later

Diane remains at **Mass General, Room 372**, comatose. She has not regained responsiveness. [[Player Characters/Leonard Holloway\|Leonard]] has visited her every day during his long rest, and she has shown no change. [[Player Characters/Chandler T. Harrow\|Chandler]] has connected the *cold-that-wasn't-weather* on her skin to the three incidents in [[NPCs/Boston Police Department/Detective O'Malley\|Detective O'Malley]]'s new file — something is making unawakened people cold in ways that aren't meteorological. Whether [[NPCs/Boston Paranormal Society/Diane Okoye\|Diane]] will recover, and what the Ghost Captain actually left in her, remain open questions.
