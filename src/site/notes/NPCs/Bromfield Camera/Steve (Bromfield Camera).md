# Steve

> [!info] NPC Summary
> **Role:** Owner of [[Locations/Bromfield Camera\|Bromfield Camera]]
> **Affiliation:** Bromfield Camera, downtown Boston
> **Location:** Bromfield Camera storefront
> **First Appeared:** [[Sessions/Session 03 - Squiggly Bigglies\|Session 03 - Squiggly Bigglies]]

## Description

**Appearance:** Not on-screen described in detail. Reading the era and the shop: 1980s small-business owner, comfortable behind a glass counter, smooth with payment plans. *(Out-of-character: the GM modeled Steve on his actual Uncle Steve, who ran Bromfield Camera until a couple of years before the campaign began.)*

**Personality:** Patient. Hospitable. Believes in things that last — *"this shop is all about things that last"* — and won't sell you a disposable. Will throw in a roll of film free if you make a good buying decision. Genuinely interested in budding photographers.

**Voice / Mannerisms:** Polite. Welcoming. Slips into salesman cadence when describing camera features. *"Now over here, we have the Polaroid One Step."*

---

## Background

Steve owns [[Locations/Bromfield Camera\|Bromfield Camera]], an old camera shop in downtown Boston that has operated continuously since 1910. Green-paneled storefront, yellow sign, glass counters. He stocks Kodak, Polaroid, and a range of older 35mm equipment. He offers payment plans on bigger purchases. He calls customers back when their special-orders arrive.

## Session 03

The party stopped in after the Walgreens run. [[Player Characters/Leonard Holloway\|Leonard]] was in the market for an entry-level camera. Steve pulled out the **Kodak Star 35mm** and the **Polaroid One Step** — the two cheapest in stock — and laid out the trade-offs:

> *"A roll of Kodak 200 would be three bucks for a roll, and you get a ton of pictures out of that. One of the Polaroid packs gives you just ten pictures, and it's nine bucks. That's almost a dollar a picture — but you get it right away."*
> — **Steve**

[[Player Characters/Leonard Holloway\|Leonard]] was short. [[Player Characters/Chandler T. Harrow\|Chandler]] **slid a $20 in front of him with sleight of hand** as if Leonard had dropped it. Steve, focused on Leonard, didn't catch it. (Only [[Player Characters/Jacques le Coq\|Jacques]] noticed, and he matched the gift with a separate $20.)

[[Player Characters/Leonard Holloway\|Leonard]] walked out with a Polaroid One Step. Steve threw in a free first roll of film "*because you've impressed me with your choice.*"

[[Player Characters/Chandler T. Harrow\|Chandler]] also asked about a small set of binoculars in the $20–30 range. Steve took his callback number and said he'd ring when something appropriate came in.
