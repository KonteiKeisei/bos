## Background

Dennis was the witness to the **first** of the three confirmed Cold Incidents on the night of **October 29, 1986** at 912 Massachusetts Avenue in Cambridge. He was hospitalized at **Cambridge City Hospital** and discharged by the time [[NPCs/Boston Police Department/Detective O'Malley\|Detective O'Malley]] tried to follow up. The detective's notes on Dennis are thinner than the other two incident reports — Dennis got out before he could be properly questioned.

The party has not yet interviewed him.

---

## What's in O'Malley's File

The Fahey interview notes are partial. O'Malley's file confirms:

- The incident took place at his address on October 29, evening
- He was admitted to Cambridge City Hospital
- He left the hospital before the detective could complete the interview
- The report is dated October 29 with supplementary contact info attached

The party has his home address and phone number via the file.
