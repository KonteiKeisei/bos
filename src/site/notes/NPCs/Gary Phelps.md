# Gary Phelps

![[Gary Phelps.webp]]

> [!info] NPC Summary
> **Role:** Member, Boston Paranormal Society
> **Affiliation:** Boston Paranormal Society
> **Location:** Boston — Society headquarters (community college basement)
> **First Appeared:** [[Session 01 - The Convergence]]

## Description

**Appearance:** Mid-fifties, perpetually a little rumpled, the kind of man who has a flask on him at all times and doesn't particularly try to hide it. Red Sox cap. Always a Red Sox cap.

**Personality:** Sceptical of almost everything — including, sometimes, the Society he's been a member of for fifteen years. More of a talker than an investigator. Still shows up, though. That has to count for something.

**Voice / Mannerisms:** Grumbles. Has strong opinions about the Red Sox that he will share whether you asked or not. Uses humour to avoid taking anything too seriously, including actual supernatural events.

---

## Background

Gary joined the Boston Paranormal Society under circumstances he no longer likes to explain. He mostly attends meetings, complains about the Red Sox, and occasionally helps carry equipment to sites that other people investigate.

He was not present at Fort Point Channel on the night of October 10th. He was, in all likelihood, somewhere complaining about Game 7.
