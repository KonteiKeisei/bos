# Patricia Osei

![Osei.webp](/img/user/Images/NPCs/Osei.webp)

> [!info] NPC Summary
> **Role:** Harvard affiliate; witness to the Soldier's Monument Cold Incident (Incident 3)
> **Affiliation:** Harvard
> **Location:** Lives on Beacon Street; works at Harvard
> **First Appeared:** [[Sessions/Session 03 - Squiggly Bigglies\|Session 03 - Squiggly Bigglies]] (phone call; dinner appointment pending)

## Description

**Appearance:** Not seen on-screen yet. [[NPCs/Boston Common/Ranger Diggs\|Ranger Diggs]] described her as *"real fine."* Per [[NPCs/Harvard/Patricia Osei\|Patricia]]'s own self-description, she is **vegan** — *"I do not let any animal products enter this temple that is my body."*

**Personality:** Bougie. Academic. Particular. Does not suffer the South Shore Diner. Refers to herself in academic register and expects the same in return.

**Voice / Mannerisms:** Precise. Punctual. Closed her phone call with "Watson out."

---

## Background

Patricia is a Harvard affiliate (employee or trustee — the party doesn't know which yet). She lives on Beacon Street, not far from [[Player Characters/Chandler T. Harrow\|Chandler]]. On the morning of **November 3, 1986**, she was jogging through Boston Common past the Soldier's Monument when she felt the cold drop and saw the **frost-script** appearing on the monument. She was sketching it from memory when [[NPCs/Boston Common/Ranger Diggs\|Ranger Diggs]] arrived to investigate the shouting and photographed it with his Polaroid.

She gave her name and statement to Diggs, who passed it to Boston PD. [[NPCs/Boston Police Department/Detective O'Malley\|Detective O'Malley]] later interviewed her in person, dated November 9.

Patricia is the **only Cold Incident witness who did not lose consciousness, go foggy, or describe any subjective effect** from the Enchantment. The party's working theory: she resisted it. [[Player Characters/Bohdan Thorruk\|Bo]]'s read on it:

> *"Maybe because she's a little bit more than human."*
> — **[[Player Characters/Bohdan Thorruk\|Bo]]**

## Session 03 — The Phone Call

[[Player Characters/Chandler T. Harrow\|Chandler]] tried Harvard's front office, got transferred to Public Relations (because he identified himself as press), tried to deceive his way back to her home line, and failed catastrophically. Harvard PR directed him to her home phone. He called her there directly.

She declined his proposed venue.

> *"I will meet you. Why don't you meet me at the Cafe Pamplona over in Cambridge? When would you like to do this?"*
> — **Patricia Osei**

> *"This evening, if possible."*
> — **[[Player Characters/Chandler T. Harrow\|Chandler]]**

> *"I can do seven."*
> — **Patricia Osei**

She closed the call with deliberate theatre, after [[Player Characters/Chandler T. Harrow\|Chandler]] tried to match her academic register with *"indubitably":*

> *"Mr. Holmes, I will see you at Cafe Pamplona at 7 p.m. Watson out."*
> — **Patricia Osei**
