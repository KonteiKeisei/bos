## Background

Gerard owns and operates [[Locations/Boval\|Boval]], the French bakery on the ground floor of the building where he also rents out flats upstairs. [[Player Characters/Pierre-Jackson Laferrière\|Pierre]] has lived above the bakery for some time. [[Player Characters/Jacques le Coq\|Jacques]] is Gerard's tenant as well, paying him rent on a unit in the building.

Gerard and his daughter [[NPCs/Bova/Marceline\|Marceline]] heard the story of [[Player Characters/Pierre-Jackson Laferrière\|Pierre]]'s supposed death after the events of [[Sessions/Session 01 - The Convergence\|Session 01 - The Convergence]]. Both believed him gone. Both were visibly relieved when he returned.
