# Detective O'Malley

> [!info] NPC Summary
> **Role:** Boston Police Department Detective
> **Affiliation:** Boston Police Department
> **Location:** Boston
> **First Appeared:** [[Sessions/Session 01 - The Convergence\|Session 01 - The Convergence]]

## Description

**Appearance:** Somewhere in his late forties, the kind of face that's seen too many crime scenes without enough sleep. Wears the same coat. Drinks bad coffee. Gets the job done.

**Personality:** Weary, professional, quietly decent. The kind of cop who will bend a rule if it means the right thing happens, but won't break one without a good reason. Has known Chandler long enough to know he's going to be a problem and help him anyway.

**Voice / Mannerisms:** Direct. Brief. Doesn't repeat himself. The threat to arrest Chandler if he's found at another crime scene was delivered without raising his voice — which made it more convincing, not less.

---

## Background

O'Malley has been quietly feeding information to Chandler Harrow about the drowning deaths in Boston Harbor — Wesley Drummond, Roy Tillman, Peter Vance — all found under circumstances that don't add up. He's pulling files. He's not ready to call it supernatural. He's also not ready to close the cases.

He found Chandler at the Fort Point crime scene after the ghost incident. He didn't arrest him. He did make very clear that it couldn't happen again.

---

## Session 01

O'Malley contacted Chandler with leads on the drowning deaths and was present at Fort Point after Diane Okoye was taken to hospital. He recognised Chandler at the scene and warned him off — but didn't push it further. Whatever is happening at the harbour, O'Malley knows enough to know he doesn't fully understand it.
