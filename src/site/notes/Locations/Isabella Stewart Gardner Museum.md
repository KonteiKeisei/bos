# Isabella Stewart Gardner Museum

> [!info] Location Summary
> **District / Neighborhood:** Fenway-Kenmore
> **Type:** Art Museum
> **Era:** 1986
> **Status:** Mundane & Mysterious

## Description

![Isabella Stewart Gardner Museum.webp|Isabella Stewart Gardner Museum|500](/img/user/Images/Locations/Isabella%20Stewart%20Gardner%20Museum.webp)

Housed in a building styled after a 15th-century Venetian palace. Established 1903 by Isabella Stewart Gardner. Eclectic collection of European, Asian, and American art. The arrangement of the collection has remained unchanged per Gardner's specific instructions — an unusual and legally binding clause.

---

## History

Established 1903. Gardner arranged the museum to evoke the feeling of a private home. The collection's arrangement is protected by the terms of her will.

---

## Key Features

- Courtyard garden (central feature)
- Tapestries, paintings, sculptures, decorative arts
- Venetian palace architecture
- Intimate gallery rooms named after regions and eras

## Magical Significance

Gardner's insistence that nothing be moved — ever — has long been noted by the awakened as suspicious. Some believe the arrangement itself forms a protective ward or magical circuit.
