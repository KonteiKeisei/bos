# Faneuil Hall

> [!info] Location Summary
> **District / Neighborhood:** Waterfront / Downtown
> **Type:** Historic Market & Meeting Hall
> **Era:** 1986
> **Status:** Mundane

## Description

![Faneuil Hall.webp|Faneuil Hall|500](/img/user/Images/Locations/Faneuil%20Hall.webp)

Known as 'The Cradle of Liberty.' Built in 1742, funded by merchant Peter Faneuil. Patriots like Samuel Adams used it to inspire the public against British tyranny. Site of America's first Town Meeting. Surrounding marketplace remains a bustling hub of commerce.

---

## History

Built 1742. Hotbed of revolutionary ideas. Continued to serve as a gathering spot for speeches and public debates throughout American history.

---

## Key Features

- The Great Hall (meeting space)
- Grasshopper weathervane atop the building
- Surrounding Quincy Market complex
