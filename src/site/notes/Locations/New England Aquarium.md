# New England Aquarium

> [!info] Location Summary
> **District / Neighborhood:** Central Wharf / Boston Harbor
> **Type:** Marine Aquarium
> **Era:** 1986
> **Status:** Mundane & Fathomless-Adjacent

## Description

![New England Aquarium.webp|New England Aquarium|500](/img/user/Images/Locations/New%20England%20Aquarium.webp)

Established 1969. Located on Central Wharf overlooking Boston Harbor. Home to giant sea turtles, penguins, and exotic fish. Centerpiece is the Giant Ocean Tank — a four-story cylindrical coral reef exhibit with 360-degree views. Conducts marine research and conservation. Focus on ocean sustainability and marine animal rescue.

---

## History

Opened 1969. Expanded its research and conservation programs through the 1970s and 80s.

---

## Key Features

- Giant Ocean Tank (four-story coral reef exhibit)
- Penguin colony exhibit
- Harbor seal tank
- Marine research center

## Magical Significance

The harbor outside the Aquarium sits above unusually deep water for this part of the coast. Those attuned to the Fathomless have noted a persistent low resonance emanating from somewhere beneath the facility.
