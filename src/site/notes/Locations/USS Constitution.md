# USS Constitution

> [!info] Location Summary
> **District / Neighborhood:** Charlestown Navy Yard
> **Type:** Historic Warship / Museum
> **Era:** 1986
> **Status:** Mundane

## Description

![USS Constitution.webp|USS Constitution|500](/img/user/Images/Locations/USS%20Constitution.webp)

The oldest commissioned warship afloat in the world. Launched 1797. Known as 'Old Ironsides' for her resilience in the War of 1812. Located at the Charlestown Navy Yard. Museum explores naval history, early American sea power, and the lives of sailors who served aboard.

---

## History

Launched 1797. Earned 'Old Ironsides' nickname in War of 1812 engagements. Still a commissioned U.S. Navy vessel.

---

## Key Features

- The ship itself (accessible for tours)
- Museum with historical artifacts and interactive exhibits
- Charlestown Navy Yard waterfront
