# Café Pamplona

> [!info] Location Summary
> **District / Neighborhood:** Cambridge — 12 Bow Street, near Harvard Square
> **Type:** Coffee house / café
> **Era:** 1986 *(closed in real life decades later; alive and busy in-campaign)*
> **Status:** Active; venue for the [[NPCs/Harvard/Patricia Osei\|Patricia Osei]] interview

## Description

A small, low-ceilinged coffee house near Harvard Square. Quiet, academic, the kind of place where graduate students nurse a single espresso for three hours and the staff doesn't push them out. Vegetarian-friendly menu. No alcohol. Reputation for being one of the oldest coffee houses in Cambridge.

[[NPCs/Harvard/Patricia Osei\|Patricia Osei]] chose it specifically. *"I will meet you. Why don't you meet me at the Cafe Pamplona over in Cambridge?"*
