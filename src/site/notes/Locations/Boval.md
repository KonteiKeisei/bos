# Boval

> [!info] Location Summary
> **District / Neighborhood:** Boston — below [[Player Characters/Pierre-Jackson Laferrière\|Pierre]]'s apartment
> **Type:** French bakery
> **Era:** 1986
> **Status:** Active / mundane

## Description

A small neighborhood bakery on the ground floor of the building where [[Player Characters/Pierre-Jackson Laferrière\|Pierre]] rents his flat. Run by a patron and his daughter, **[[NPCs/Bova/Marceline\|Marceline]]**. Known in the neighborhood for fresh croissants and the kind of quiet loyalty that only family businesses develop.

When [[Player Characters/Pierre-Jackson Laferrière\|Pierre]] was pronounced dead in the harbor the night of the Convergence, the patron and his daughter grieved. When he walked back in ten days later, they refused to charge him for anything, which is how this campaign discovered that Pierre has two people in Boston who actually like him.

---

## Key Features

- Street-level bakery, apartment stairs just adjacent
- Phone number reaches Marceline — *"Hello, you have reached Boval. This is Marceline speaking. How may I help you? Would you like to place an order?"*
- Fresh croissants
- Sugary pastries (Pierre's preferred haul)
- A daughter who is not anyone's maid for free
