# Bromfield Camera

> [!info] Location Summary
> **District / Neighborhood:** Downtown Boston
> **Type:** Independent camera shop
> **Era:** Established 1910 *(real-world establishment the GM's actual uncle owned until recent years)*
> **Status:** Active; owner-operated by [[NPCs/Bromfield Camera/Steve (Bromfield Camera)\|Steve]]

## Description

A small, family-run camera shop in downtown Boston that has operated continuously since 1910. Recognizable from the street by its **green paneled wall** and **yellow sign** reading *BROMFIELD CAMERA.* Old wooden front door, slightly ajar during business hours. Glass display cases, a working antique cash register, and well-organized stock — Kodak, Polaroid, 35mm gear, accessories, film stock, and an order book for special requests.

[[NPCs/Bromfield Camera/Steve (Bromfield Camera)\|Steve]] runs the floor personally. He'll talk through any camera in stock, offer payment plans on bigger purchases, and call you back when a custom order comes in.

> *"This shop is all about things that last."*
> — **[[NPCs/Bromfield Camera/Steve (Bromfield Camera)\|Steve]]**
