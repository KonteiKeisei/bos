# Museum of Fine Arts (MFA)

> [!info] Location Summary
> **District / Neighborhood:** Fenway-Kenmore
> **Type:** Art Museum
> **Era:** 1986
> **Status:** Mundane & Arcane

## Description

![[Museum of Fine Arts (MFA).webp|Museum of Fine Arts|500]]

Established 1870. One of the largest museums in the United States. Nearly 500,000 works spanning ancient Egyptian artifacts to contemporary art. Notable for Asian art, French Impressionism, and American colonial-era art. Architecture expanded by I.M. Pei and Norman Foster.

---

## History

Founded 1870. Expanded significantly through the 20th century.

---

## Key Features

- Evans Wing (American art)
- Classical antiquities
- Asian art collection
- Impressionist gallery

## Magical Significance

Certain works in the MFA's collection are not what they appear to be. The awakened community is quietly aware that some pieces were donated by individuals with connections to civic magic — and that a few artworks function as anchors or seals for things best left undisturbed.
