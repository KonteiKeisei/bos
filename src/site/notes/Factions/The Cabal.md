# The Cabal

> [!info] Faction Summary
> **Type:** Mortal criminal conspiracy — Boston elite
> **Disposition:** Hostile
> **Headquarters:** Unknown; connections through Red Sox ownership and Boston finance
> **Leader:** [[NPCs/Harold Whitmore\|Harold Whitmore]] *(public-facing)*
> **Status:** Active; covering their tracks after the pact betrayal

## Overview

The Cabal is the group of Boston's elite who understood the Fenway Pact and chose to break it for profit.

For decades, they have been **farming Boston's suffering** — engineering near-misses, sustaining the city's almost-winning, and harvesting the grief and desperate devotion that followed. The engine of heartbreak was profitable: ticket sales, merchandise, civic identity, cultural currency. The compact with the Fey Courts was the engine. The Cabal chose to keep it running past its due date.

When it became clear that 1986 was the year the pact expired — that Boston was meant to win, the Fey Courts would revel, and the compact would end — the Cabal made a deliberate choice. They paid to betray it.

Bill Buckner was paid to fumble the ball.

- Contain the supernatural fallout from breaking the Fey compact
