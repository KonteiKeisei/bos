# The Fenway Pact

> [!info] The Core Truth
> Boston wasn't cursed. It was **bound by a bargain** — and that bargain was deliberately, criminally broken.

---

## The Land Beneath the Park

The site now occupied by Fenway Park was once the **Back Fenn** — a liminal wetland where the Material Plane pressed thin against the Feywild. This was not incidental. The land was chosen because it was *already* a place of intersection, where mortal grief and Fey hunger could naturally feed one another.

When Fenway Park was built in **1912**, its ownership allowed the Fey to operate within the park's boundaries — an informal permission that set the stage for what came next.

---

## The Compact of 1926

After the sale of Babe Ruth, a **formal compact** was sealed with both the **Winter Court** and the **Summer Court**.

The terms:
- For **sixty years**, the Red Sox would be shaped by the Park's magic.
- Victory would remain just out of reach. The city would suffer — but it would *care deeply*.
- In the **sixtieth year (1986)**, Boston was meant to **win the World Series**.
- The Fey would revel openly for a single night. The pact would end. The debt would be paid.

The near-misses — 1946, 1967, 1975 — were not accidents. They were **engineered**, the product of a decades-long arrangement between mortal power and Fey hunger. Boston's suffering was the engine. The almost-winning was the product.

---

## The Betrayal — October 25, 1986

On **October 25, 1986**, the compact was deliberately broken.

**Bill Buckner was paid to fumble the ball.**

The betrayal was systemic — a business decision made by the men who had been farming Boston's grief for decades, choosing to preserve the profitable engine of almost-winning rather than honor a sixty-year compact with inhuman powers.

In Fey culture, this is not merely a breach of contract. It is a **capital crime**.

---

## The Courts' Response

**Winter** answered with **judgment** — cold, deliberate, patient. Winter does not rage. Winter calculates. Its response will come in measured steps, each one colder than the last.

**Summer** answered with **fury** — immediate, volcanic, uncontrolled. Summer's designated instrument for the night of celebration was the **Green Monster**.

---

## The Green Monster

The Green Monster is not a Winter creation. It is a **Summer Court titan** — a creature of unchecked growth designed to rampage for a single night of joy, then sleep again beneath the left-field wall.

Denied its purpose, the Green Monster rises now as something **warped and catastrophic**. It was never meant to hold this energy. Without the release of victory, without the joyous revel it was built for, it has been festering for weeks. What wakes is no longer the joyful giant of Summer's design — it is something broken by denial, enormous in its grief, and without any channel for what it carries.


---

## The Cabal

The men who broke the pact did not act in ignorance. They understood exactly what they were doing. See: [[Factions/The Cabal\|The Cabal]]
