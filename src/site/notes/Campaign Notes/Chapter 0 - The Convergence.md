# Chapter 0 — The Convergence

> [!info] Chapter Info
> **Subtitle:** "Something Woke Up"
> **Timeframe:** Late October 1986 — The Night After Game 7
> **Tone:** Melancholy, unsettling, building dread
> **Session Length:** ~3–4 hours
> **Party Level:** 3

---

## Chapter Overview

Boston is grieving.

The Red Sox have lost the World Series in the most heartbreaking fashion imaginable. Bill Buckner's error in Game 6 became a funeral. Game 7 was just the burial. The city feels hollowed out — bars that should be celebrating are silent. Streets that should be filled with joy are empty. Something that was supposed to happen didn't, and the absence is palpable.

Tonight, six strangers will be drawn toward the harbor by forces they don't yet understand. By dawn, they will have faced something impossible together — and nothing will ever be the same.

## Key NPCs

### [[NPCs/The Grief-Eater\|The Grief-Eater]] *(Encountered)*

A minor Fey entity that slipped through a crack in the veil opened by Boston's collective despair. Not malevolent by nature — simply hungry, and Boston is a feast.

**Behavior:** Feeds on grief, causes localized hauntings, whispers fragments of lost hopes. Non-aggressive until cornered. When threatened, lashes out with waves of despair.

**Key Line (when defeated):**
> *"The harvest was denied. The courts remember. Winter judges. Summer rages. The Green One dreams of walking..."*

Harbor Dogs member. Pierre's closest friend in Boston. Located at The Rusty Anchor bar, South Boston.

## Party Introduction Beats

Each character is drawn to the harbor by different means:

**[[Player Characters/Pierre-Jackson Laferrière\|Pierre-Jackson Laferrière]]** — His Harbor Dogs connections; Matty Callahan's cryptic warning about strange things at the docks.

**[[Player Characters/Jacques le Coq\|Jacques le Coq]]** — Suspended from the Bruins after his ejection. Wandering the city with nowhere to be, he follows the feeling that something is wrong in his new city.

**[[Player Characters/Chandler T. Harrow\|Chandler T. Harrow]]** — Following a story. Unusual reports near the waterfront that nobody else at the Globe seems interested in covering.

**[[Player Characters/Leonard Holloway\|Leonard Holloway]]** — Drawn by anomalous readings on equipment of his own construction.

**[[Player Characters/Bo\|Bo]]** — The natural world is disturbed. Something has shifted in the patterns of the city's living things.
